Achievements

Toyota has won several honors and awards, including:

  • Toyota was awarded the Deming Prize in 1965.
  • Toyota Motor Company won Japan's first “Quality Control Award” in the early 1970s.
  • In 1999, Toyota Australia was awarded the General Motors Safety Medal.
  • The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency has awarded Toyota Engineering & Industrial North America a 2007 Energy Star Award.
  • Toyota won the Green Car Maker Award in 2008

Toyota has achieved several records throughout its journey, including:

  • Toyota is first in the world in terms of the number of units produced, as its production reached 9,497,754 units in 2007.
  • Toyota Corolla is the best car and best-selling vehicle in history, with more than 31.6 million units sold since its launch in 1966 AD until 2005 AD during 9 generations.
  • Toyota Swarrier had the first GPS system from the factory in 1991.
