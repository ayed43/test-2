Germany cars

The companies that own German cars, led by BMW, Mercedes Benz, Volkswagen, and Audi, have a long history in the automobile industry, built with the fame of German machines that have swept the world since the 1940s until now. German cars have powerful engines and bodies, as well as luxury features and amenities inside the cabin, but the prices of these cars and their spare parts are high, making some, especially in the Arab region, knocking on the doors of the Japanese market or even the American, Italian, Korean, and most recently the Chinese market.
